How it works

Record once, replay forever

Three steps. The same loop you see in the icon — record, tweak, replay.

  1. 01

    Record

    Tap, swipe, drag — anything you can do with one finger. Touch Recorder watches and remembers.

  3. 02

    Tweak

    Pick playback speed, repeats, and the wait time between runs. Change any of them mid-playback.

  5. 03

    Replay

    Hit Play. Floating controls stay on top of any app. Play, Stop, or jump back to recording in one tap.

Permissions

What we ask for, and why

We don't sell your information. Your recordings stay on your phone.

  • Accessibility Lets the app tap the screen for you. Without it, no auto-tap app can work on a phone that is not rooted.
  • Display over other apps So the floating control buttons can sit on top of your game or app.
  • Notifications Shows a small status while a recording is playing, so you always know it is still running.
  • Device Admin (optional) Used only if you turn on "Stop and lock" — locks your screen when a stop rule fires.
Great for

Anything you'd rather not tap a hundred times yourself

  • 1 Mobile games — daily logins, idle taps, claim buttons, repeat farming.
  • 2 Long forms or surveys you have to fill out the same way every time.
  • 3 Anyone with limited hand mobility who wants help with repeated taps.
  • 4 Any task where the answer is "tap that, then that, then that, again".
