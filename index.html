§02 · how it works

Record once, replay forever.

Three steps. Same loop you see in the icon.

  1. 01 / record

    Record.

    Tap, swipe, drag. Touch Recorder watches via Accessibility and saves the gesture stream.

  2. 02 / tweak

    Tweak.

    Pick speed, repeats, wait between runs. Change any of them mid-playback. No restart.

  3. 03 / replay

    Replay.

    Hit Play. Floating controls stay on top of any app. Stop or jump back to record in one tap.

§06 · permissions

What gets asked, and why.

Recordings stay on the device. No data sale. Network calls are limited to ads and billing.

  • Accessibility Lets the app dispatch taps for you. Required for non-rooted touch dispatch. No alternative on stock Android.
  • Display over other apps So the floating controls sit on top of your game or app. TYPE_APPLICATION_OVERLAY. 48dp circles. Drag-positioned.
  • Notifications Shows a small status while a recording is playing. Foreground-service notification. Required by Android 13+.
  • Device Admin optional Used only with the "stop and lock" rule (Premium). Locks screen via DevicePolicyManager when a stop rule fires.
